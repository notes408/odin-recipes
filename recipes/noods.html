Noods

Description

Quick and Easy addition to Seton's Weekday meals!

Have on it's own or pair with Seton's Chiggins and Broccs!

Ingredients

  • Plain Ramen Noodles from Nobrand
  • Sriracha
  • Sesame Oil
  • Optional: Chili/Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil Ramen Noodles in hot boiling water until noodles soften slightly.
  2. Put Ramen noodles in a strainer and let the water drain
  3. Pour noodles back into the pot or a bowl
  4. Put a TSP of sesame oil and mix with the noodles.
  5. Put Sriracha Sauce and mix in with the noodles
  6. Can add Chili Oil and or Soy Sauce for extra flavor
  7. Enjoy!
