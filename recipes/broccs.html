Broccs

Description

Another one of Seton's most trusted meals for his weekday healths!

Pair with Seton's beloved Chiggins!

Ingredients

  • Fresh/Frozen Broccoli
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Good attitude

Steps

  1. Cut Broccoli into individual Florets
  2. You can also keep the stem and cut into small pieces
  3. Boil Water in Pot until it bubbles
  4. Put Broccoli into pot and let sit for 3~5 minutes
  5. Put Broccoli in a strainer and let dry and cool for a few minutes
  6. Enjoy!
