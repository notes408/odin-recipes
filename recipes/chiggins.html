Seton's Meal Prep

Description

One of Seton's most trusted and used meals for his weekday needs!

Inspired by Chipotle's own chicken, Seton researched an easy and budget-friendly way to replicate this popular dish.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Chicken Breast
  • Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce (Any Brand)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Defrost Chicken Breast
  2. Pour Chipotle Peppers in Mixing Bowl
